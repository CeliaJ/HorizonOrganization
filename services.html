Services

People get the world running but the youth holds the key to progression and their education, welfare and employment becomes a great factor for every country. But these can not be achieved without ensuring the welfare of the family itself and hence emerges the needs for primary healthcare and proper awareness of every situation we encounter.

Realizing this Horizon organization, beginning in the corridors of education, has adopted a lifecycle approach with intensive programmes focused on family health, livelihood and community engagement through women empowerment, which addresses the needs of less privileged children, their families and the larger community.

Horizon organization has launched three new programs and platforms to help support people to tackle the problem of healthcare, education and unemployment during this pandemic:

About us

Horizon is an organization based in India directly benefitting over 15,00,000 families every year. It was established in 2009, since then it has grown from a group of volunteers to a huge international network of students, working professionals, homemakers and senior citizens. Horizon believes in providing community development for young people to have better and more realistic opportunities, for processing into higher education or gaining access to progressive employment opportunities. Horizon was started by a group of volunteers lead by Celia Joyce, who wanted to bring a change in the lives of people by bringing health and education to their doorsteps. This includes provision of primary health care as well as imparting awareness to the population through improvement of literacy status of individuals and income generation activities.

Our Mission

Horizon Organization focuses on building a community which would help to develop a society in which individuals are provided with equal opportunities to access health and educational facilities and help each other grow together in harmony.

Our Vision

The future vision of horizon is to expand its reach worldwide and bring a change in the lives of people we touch.

Horizon Organization

Horizon is an organization based in India directly benefitting over 15,00,000 families every year. It was established in 2009, since then it has grown from a group of volunteers to a huge international network of students, working professionals, homemakers and senior citizens. Horizon believes in providing community development for young people to have better and more realistic opportunities, for processing into higher education or gaining access to progressive employment opportunities.

The COVID-19 pandemic has led to a dramatic loss of human life worldwide and presents an unprecedented challenge to public health, food systems and the world of work. Millions of people are facing a risk of falling into extreme poverty while a lot others are facing serious unemployment issues The majority lack social protection and access to quality healthcare.

During these stressful times we at Horizon org have decided to help the society grow and play its role in building the economy back by introducing our new ventures Edu., Pulse and InvestiGo. Our main focus is to provide services to three sectors, Education, Heathcare and Unemployment.

One by One Program:

Amid the virus outbreak, Horizon took up the primary healthcare provision of Haryana, its responsibility. The organization was to make a visible impact 2.5 months after the launch of the program. New temporary health clinics were set up along with delivery of medicines online. Moreover, the NGO gave out free covid-19 test which could be booked from the ease of just a click sitting home. Along with providing the necessities they also spread awareness of the do’s and don’ts in the rural areas of Haryana.
